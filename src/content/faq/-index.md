faqs:
  - title: What documents do I need for tax preparation?
    answer: For individual tax returns, bring your W-2s, 1099s, previous year's tax return, receipts for deductible expenses, and identification. Business owners should also bring profit and loss statements, bank statements, and business expense receipts. We'll provide a complete checklist when you schedule your appointment to ensure we have everything needed for accurate tax preparation.

Contact us and mention "Group Discounts" during your call to learn more about our special offers and package deals tailored to your organization's needs.

  - title: How much do your bookkeeping services cost?
    answer: Our professional bookkeeping services are charged at competitive hourly rates. We believe in transparent pricing and will provide you with a detailed quote based on your specific business needs. Each business has unique requirements, so we customize our services accordingly. Contact us for a free consultation to discuss your bookkeeping needs and receive a personalized quote.

    answer: Our comprehensive payroll management service includes an initial setup fee of $250 (one-time fee) and a monthly fee of $50 for ongoing payroll administration. Please note that additional fees may be charged directly from ADP Payroll Processing Company for their processing services.

  - title: Can you help with sales tax preparation and filing?
    answer: Absolutely! We provide comprehensive sales tax preparation services for businesses of all sizes. Our team ensures accurate calculation of sales tax obligations, proper documentation, and timely filing to help you stay compliant with state and local tax requirements. We understand the complexities of sales tax regulations and will handle all the details for you.

  - title: How far in advance should I schedule my tax appointment?
    answer: We recommend scheduling your tax preparation appointment as early as possible, especially during tax season (January through April). We offer online booking services for your convenience, making it easy to secure your preferred appointment time.
